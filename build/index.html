What is ULTIMATE Cart Drawer?

ULTIMATE cart drawer replaces the boring old cart page with a slide out cart drawer. Let the customers stay on the product page when they add the product to cart. The app has 4 default themes available which can be further customized to match your store branding.

ULTIMATE cart drawer also lets you upsell products, offer rewards such as free shipping, discounts, BOGO offers, and more when users reach a certain amount in their cart. A progress bar displays at the top of the cart drawer showing shoppers how much is remaining before they unlock the rewards.
